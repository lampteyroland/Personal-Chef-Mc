We bring you the best Private Chef cooking experience you ever imagine.


McDonald, Personal Chef & Catering has a great abstract spin on food which comes from various cultures and ethnic backgrounds. Born and raised in Ghana, Chef is known for delivering delicious, nutritious, personalized meals for you and your family.

Through Knowledge and experiences gained finest Luxury establishment such as Flair catering, Labadi Beach Hotel, Kempinski GoLD Coast, he has always had always had the urge of aspiring to inspire which Led him in the creation of a world class gastronomic experience, encompassing the finest quality of food and an unmatched personalized service for private and corporate clients.We provide personal chef experience and catering for any type of occasion. Inspiring authentic flavors and bring perfection to your dining table each and every time Regardless of the genre or culture, he is on hand for preparing meals both large and small in distinctly elegant or simple locations.

Chef McDonald Prides Himself in Using Only the Highest Quality, Freshest, Seasonal Ingredients. You Can Taste the Quality. You Can Taste the Freshness. You Can Taste the Difference.
